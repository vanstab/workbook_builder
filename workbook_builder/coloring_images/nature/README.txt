📁 NATURE FOLDER

Add coloring images of natural things here!

Examples:
- flower.png
- tree.png
- leaf.png
- cloud.png
- rainbow.png
- sun.png

Just drag and drop PNG, JPG, or PDF files into this folder.
They will automatically appear in your coloring worksheets!

See _HOW_TO_ADD_IMAGES.txt in the parent folder for detailed instructions.
